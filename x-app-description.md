**Project Name:** Daily Chip

**Description (100+ words):**

Daily Chip is an AI-powered content project that publishes one educational PDF deep dive every day. The AI character (Chip, a sarcastic chipmunk) researches and writes a 30-minute read on a specific topic, then shares it on X along with key takeaways. The project aims to make learning more accessible by condensing complex topics into digestible daily content. Topics cover self-improvement, productivity, career growth, finance, AI, and skills development. The account engages with followers, answers questions, and provides actionable advice. It's both an educational platform and an experiment in AI-driven content creation and automation.

**Use Case:**
- Automated daily posting of educational content
- AI-generated research and writing
- Community engagement and Q&A
